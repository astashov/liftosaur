1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Information automatically collected

In Short: Some information - such as IP address and/or browser and device characteristics - is collected automatically when you visit our Services.

This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information, such as your IP address, browser and device characteristics, operating system, language preferences, referring URLs, device name, country, location, information about how and when you use our Services and other technical information. 2. GOOGLE HEALTH CONNECT INTEGRATION

Our app integrates with Google Health Connect to access and store specific health-related data, including body weight and body fat. Here's how we handle this data:

1. Data Collection

We collect Health Connect data through the Health Connect integration to enhance the app's functionality. This data is used solely for tracking and providing insights within the app.

2. Data Storage

The collected data is stored locally on your device and in the cloud on our servers. We implement strict security measures to protect this data from unauthorized access, ensuring it is stored safely and securely.

3. Data Usage

The data collected from Health Connect is not used for any purpose other than to provide app functionality. We do not use this data for marketing or advertising.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your health data to third parties. Your data is private and is only accessible within the app for your use.

5. User Control

You retain control over your data. You can choose to delete or update your body weight and body fat data at any time within the app. If you wish to delete your data stored on our servers, you can request this through the app's settings.

6. Compliance

We comply with all applicable privacy laws and regulations related to the use and storage of health data.

6. ANALYTICS AND ADVERTISING TECHNOLOGIES

We use analytics and advertising services to understand how visitors interact with Liftosaur and to improve our marketing. These include:

  • Google Analytics 4: Tracks anonymized site usage, device information, and engagement. We have enabled IP anonymization. You can opt out via Google’s opt-out browser add-on.
  • Reddit Pixel: Measures ad performance and helps us reach people who have visited our site. You can manage ad personalization in your Reddit account settings.
  • AppsFlyer: Tracks app installs and user engagement to attribute downloads and optimize ads. We do not share identifiable data with third parties.

If you are located in the European Economic Area or UK, you may see a cookie banner requesting consent before we enable analytics or advertising cookies.
