Liftosaur Support

Need help with Liftosaur? We're happy to assist with any questions, bug reports, or feedback.

Email

Contact us at info@liftosaur.com. We usually respond within 1-2 business days.

Discord

Join our community on Discord for help from the developer and other users, program discussions, and announcements.

Documentation

Guides for the app and the Liftoscript programming language are available at www.liftosaur.com/docs.
