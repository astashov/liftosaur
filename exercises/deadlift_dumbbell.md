### Starting Position
1. **Stand with feet** shoulder-width apart.
2. **Position Dumbbells** beside your feet, palms facing your body.

### Grip
3. **Bend at the hips and knees**, lowering your torso to grasp the dumbbells with both hands.

### Setup
4. **Keep your back straight** and chest lifted, ensuring your spine is neutral.
5. **Engage your core** and set your shoulders back.

### Lift
6. **Drive through your heels**, extending your hips and knees simultaneously.
7. **Stand up tall**, fully extending your hips and locked knees without leaning back.

### Lowering
8. **Hinge at the hips** to lower the dumbbells back to the ground, maintaining a flat back.
9. **Bend your knees** as the weights pass them to return to the starting position.

### Key Points
- Keep the dumbbells close to your body throughout the movement.
- Avoid rounding your back; focus on maintaining a neutral spine.
- Control the movement on the way down to avoid injury.
