### Setup:
1. **Equipment**: Dumbbells and a preacher bench.
2. **Positioning**: Sit on the preacher bench, ensuring your upper arms rest on the angled pad.

### Execution:
1. **Grip**: Hold a dumbbell in each hand with an underhand grip (palms facing upward).
2. **Starting Position**: Let your arms extend fully, with your elbows against the pad.
3. **Curling**: 
   - Engage your biceps and curl the dumbbells toward your shoulders.
   - Keep your upper arms stationary against the pad.
4. **Peak Contraction**: Squeeze the biceps at the top of the movement.
5. **Lowering**:
   - Slowly extend your arms back to the starting position, maintaining control.
   - Avoid locking out your elbows to keep tension on the biceps.

### Tips:
- Maintain a straight back and avoid using your shoulders.
- Focus on a slow and controlled movement throughout the exercise.
- Adjust the bench height to ensure a comfortable range of motion.
