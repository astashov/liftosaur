## Starting Position
1. **Feet Placement**: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart.
2. **Grip the Ball**: Hold the medicine ball with both hands, arms extended overhead.

## Execution
1. **Engage Core**: Tighten your abdominal muscles.
2. **Lower the Ball**: Bend at the hips and knees to bring the ball down forcefully.
3. **Toss Downward**: Slam the ball down to the ground in front of you, using your core and upper body for power.
4. **Follow Through**: Allow your arms to follow the motion as you slam the ball.

## Finish
1. **Bend Knees**: Slightly bend your knees to absorb the impact.
2. **Reset**: Pick the ball up from the ground and return to the starting position for the next slam.

## Tips for Proper Form
- Keep your back straight and avoid rounding your shoulders.
- Utilize your legs and core, not just your arms, to generate power.
- Maintain a controlled motion to prevent injury.
- Focus on driving through the hips and engaging the entire body during the slam.
