## Setup
1. **Equipment:** Use a cable machine with a straight bar attachment.
2. **Adjustment:** Set the pulley at the lowest setting.
3. **Grip:** Stand facing the cable machine, grasp the bar with an overhand grip (palms facing down).

## Execution
1. **Positioning:** Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Hold the bar with arms extended down at your sides.
2. **Engage:** Keep your elbows close to your body, maintaining a slight bend in your knees.
3. **Curl:** Pull the bar upwards by flexing your elbows, keeping your wrists straight. Lift until your forearms are vertical.
4. **Control:** Slowly lower the bar back to the start position, maintaining tension in your biceps and muscles of the forearm.

## Tips
- Avoid swinging or using momentum; focus on controlled movements.
- Keep your back straight and core engaged throughout the exercise.
- Adjust the weight accordingly to maintain form.

### Set Up the Cable Machine
Attach a straight bar to the lowest pulley setting. Stand facing the machine and grasp the bar with an overhand grip (palms facing down).

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, arms extended down, and elbows close to your body with a slight knee bend.

### Return to Start
